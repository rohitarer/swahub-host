Swahub – Child Safety Standards

Swahub is a professional social platform for startup–focused individuals. We are committed to protecting children online and have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) or child sexual abuse material (CSAM).

1. Eligibility and use

Swahub is intended for users aged 16+ (or the higher age required in their country). Accounts that are suspected to belong to minors below the minimum age may be restricted or removed.

2. Zero tolerance for CSAM

The following content is strictly prohibited and leads to immediate removal and account termination: any sexualized content involving minors, grooming, sexual solicitation of minors, or links that point to such material.

3. Reporting child safety concerns

Users can report content or accounts directly inside the app and may also email rohitarer00@gmail.com with details and screenshots or links. We prioritize and review these reports as quickly as possible.

4. Enforcement and cooperation with authorities

When we believe content may constitute CSAM or exploitation of a child, we remove it, ban the account, and may preserve relevant data and report the matter to appropriate law enforcement or cybercrime authorities in the applicable jurisdiction.

5. Continuous improvement

We may update these standards as laws or best practices evolve. The latest version will always be available on this page.
