Data Deletion Policy

Effective date: November 24, 2025

Swahub respects your right to control your information. This policy describes how to request deletion of account data and what information is removed when a request is fulfilled.

Self-Service Account Deletion

  • Open the Swahub app and navigate to Settings > Delete account.
  • Confirm the company keyword and follow the prompts. Deletion requires re-authentication through Firebase.
  • Once confirmed, the account is scheduled for deletion and most data is purged within 24 hours.

Manual Deletion Requests

If you cannot access the app, submit a request through Settings > Connect Swahub Team (include the email/UID associated with your profile). Our support team will verify ownership and handle the request manually.

What Gets Deleted

  • Profile data, authentication identifiers, company information, posts, flares, reactions, and attachments stored in Supabase.
  • Private conversations in chat rooms where you are the sole participant.

Some content may remain if it belongs to other collaborators (for example, group chats, shared opportunity records) but it will no longer be linked to your identity.

Retention Exceptions

Swahub may retain limited records if required to comply with applicable laws, enforce agreements, resolve disputes, or detect abuse. Backup snapshots are removed on a rolling basis (max 30 days).

Contact

For questions about this policy or the status of a deletion request, please reach out through the in-app contact form (Settings > Connect Swahub Team).
