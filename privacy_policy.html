Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 24, 2025

Swahub (“we”, “our”, “us”) builds tools that help founders, investors, mentors, and teams connect. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you use the Swahub mobile or web application (“Services”).

Information We Collect

  • Account data — name, email address, phone, company details, and authentication identifiers provided by Firebase Authentication.
  • Profile and content — startup descriptions, mentorship requests, opportunity listings, messages, posts, flares, team member information, and attachments you voluntarily upload. Media files are stored securely using Supabase Storage.
  • Usage data — app interactions, device metadata, analytics events, and diagnostics that help us keep the platform reliable.

How We Use Information

  • Provide, personalize, and secure the Swahub experience.
  • Facilitate collaboration features such as messages, reactions, comments, and performance leaderboards.
  • Send service announcements (for example, verification emails or account alerts).
  • Improve quality through analytics, debugging, and support requests.

How We Share Information

  • With other members when you choose to publish content or interact with their profiles.
  • With infrastructure providers (Firebase, Supabase, Vercel, Google Cloud) strictly for hosting and delivery of the Services.
  • With authorities if required to comply with applicable laws or protect Swahub and its community.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Access and update profile information directly from the app at any time.
  • Download or delete data by using the in-app “Delete account” workflow or the Connect Swahub Team form.
  • Control push/email notifications from your system settings.

Data Retention & Security

Swahub stores data in MongoDB Atlas and Supabase Storage within the same geographic region as the majority of our users. We apply industry standard security practices (encryption in transit, role-based access, audit logging) and only retain personal information while your account remains active or as required for legal obligations.

Children’s Privacy

Swahub is intended for professionals 16 years of age or older. If we learn that a minor has created an account, we will remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our Services evolve. Material updates are announced in-app. Continued use of Swahub after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

Questions or privacy requests can be submitted from the Swahub mobile app via Settings > Connect Swahub Team. Our support team will respond from a verified Swahub mailbox.
